AQUA ADD-ON SYSTEMS, INC., established in September 25, 2002, is an independent organization primarily engaged in the importation, distribution and installation of water purification & treatment system for commercial, industrial and residential applications. Our company is one of the trusted in the market. Our system makes use of quality brands such as Pentek, Servtek, Aqua Pro, Fluid-O-Tech, and Pure that are recognized globally.


For industrial and commercial applications, our system is ideal for bottling, semi-conductor, hospital, pharmaceutical, centralized water supply for foods, drink processing and restaurants.

Our products include a full line of quality reverse osmosis systems, water refilling equipments, bottling equipments and bottle making machines.


We presently supply, install and service water purification and treatment in residences, restaurants and offices located in Manila, Makati, Paranaque, San Juan and Quezon City.
